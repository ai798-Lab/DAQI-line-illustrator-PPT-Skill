# Style DNA

## One Sentence

Sparse editorial poster illustration: thin black hand-drawn contour figures, oversized flat red and yellow geometric accents, a few colored dots, white or pale mint background, asymmetrical composition, and a slightly surreal magazine-poster mood.

## Visual Traits

- Human bodies, hands, faces, or limbs are drawn as loose continuous black lines.
- Anatomy is abstract and intentionally imperfect: stretched arms, simplified heads, oversized hands, looping limbs.
- Props are simple and symbolic: map, baton, pin, paper, crown, lever, frame, or path.
- Large flat accents sit behind or across the line art rather than becoming shaded objects.
- The image feels editorial and conceptual, not cute, realistic, corporate, or technical.
- Negative space is part of the composition. Empty upper or side areas are acceptable.

## Palette

- Black line: `#111111`
- Off-white background: `#F7F5EF`
- Pale mint background: `#D8E9DE`
- Red accent: `#CB3339`
- Yellow accent: `#F5D95F`
- Indigo dot: `#4A4AA0`

Use black plus two accent colors. Add indigo only as a tiny dot. Avoid gradients.

## Texture

- Keep fills flat.
- Use no shadows, 3D lighting, photographic texture, paper grain, or painterly brushwork.
- Allow slight line wobble and imperfect curves.
