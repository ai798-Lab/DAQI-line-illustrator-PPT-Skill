# Theme Translation

Convert an abstract theme into one visual metaphor. Do not illustrate every noun.

## Anchor Types

- **Era / shift**: a figure holding a red baton, lever, flag, or signal.
- **Navigation**: a yellow map with a dashed route and one red marker.
- **Power / agency**: a yellow crown, burst, or spotlight behind a line-drawn figure.
- **Control / tool use**: one oversized hand holding a red rod, key, switch, or thread.
- **Single-person enterprise**: one abstract person carrying a prop that feels bigger than them.
- **Network / coordination**: one looping line around the figure, not a node graph.

## OPC Theme Guidance

For "OPC 的时代", treat OPC as an era of one-person companies or solo operators amplified by tools:

- Use one abstract line-drawn person as the main subject.
- Give the person one red baton, lever, or signal rod to suggest control.
- Place one yellow crown, burst, or folded sheet behind the person to suggest a new era.
- Add a small dotted path or two colored dots to suggest systems around the person.
- Do not draw office buildings, laptops, robots, company logos, teams, charts, or literal business scenes.
